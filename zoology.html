51.

Collar cells are present in the members of which phyla

(1) Echinodermata

(2) Coelenterata

(3) Ctenophora

(4) Porifera
Ans. 4
Sol. Page 49, XI NCERT
Choanocytes or collar cells are the characteristic cells of poriferans and line the spongocoel.

52.

The number of ciliated rows present in Comb jellies are

(1) 8

(2) 6

(3) 4

(4) 9
Ans. 1
Sol. Page 51, XI NCERT
8 ciliated comb plates are present in comb jellies with aid in locomotion.
