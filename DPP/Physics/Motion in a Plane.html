        A boat takes two hours to travel 8 km and back in still water. If the velocity of water is 4 km/h, the time taken for going upstream 8 km and coming back is 

        (1) 2h

        (2) 2h 40 min

        (3) 1h 20 min

        (4) Cannot be estimated with the information given

          Explanation:

          (2) Boat covers distance of 16km in a still water in 2 hours.

          i.e. vB=162=8 km/hrvB=162=8 km/hr

          Now velocity of water ⇒vw=4km/hr⇒vw=4km/hr.

          Time taken for going upstream

          t1=8vB−vw=88−4=2hrt1=8vB−vw=88−4=2hr

          (As water current oppose the motion of boat)

          Time taken for going down stream

          t2=8vB+vw=88+4=812hrt2=8vB+vw=88+4=812hr

          (As water current helps the motion of boat)

          ∴ Total time =t1+t2=(2+812)hr=t1+t2=2+812hr or 2hr 40min
